OUR STORY

In 1998, in the heart of Ethiopia's Sidamo region, a group of twenty enthusiasts united to found Engida Coffee. With a shared passion for Ethiopia's coffee heritage, they embarked on a mission to bring its flavors to the world. From our humble beginnings, Engida expanded across Ethiopia's highlands, forming partnerships with local farmers committed to quality. By 2005, we showcased our beans at Seattle's Global Coffee Expo, captivating international audiences. Despite challenges like droughts and economic shifts, Engida persevered, earning a global reputation. Today, in our twenty-sixth year, we stand as a symbol of Ethiopian coffee's allure, uniting hearts worldwide. Join us as we continue our journey, one cup at a time.

WHY DO WE EXIST?

In a world where access to Ethiopia's premium coffee beans remains limited, Engida fills the gap, offering a passport to the country's diverse and unique flavors. With over 1,000 indigenous varieties, Ethiopia boasts the world's greatest genetic diversity in coffee. Engida Coffee brings this unparalleled richness to your cup, offering a journey through centuries of heritage and tradition.

WHAT WE ACHIEVED

Through fair trade partnerships, Engida Coffee has empowered over 10,000 families across Ethiopia, while also raising quality standards to ensure consistency and excellence in every bean. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices has not only preserved Ethiopia's natural beauty but also earned global recognition for the country's diverse coffee flavors. OUR VISION

At Engida Coffee, our vision extends beyond simply selling coffee. We aspire to be a driving force for positive change in Ethiopia's coffee industry and beyond. Our goals include fostering sustainable growth through environmentally friendly practices and fair trade principles. We aim to enhance the customer experience by offering high-quality products and fostering meaningful connections with our global community. Additionally, we seek to promote cultural exchange by celebrating the rich heritage of Ethiopian coffee while embracing global traditions. Innovation is key, as we continuously push the boundaries of coffee excellence through research and collaboration. Ultimately, our mission is to make a positive impact, empowering local farmers, supporting sustainability, and contributing to social initiatives wherever we go.
